1 # Narrative Text Tags # {#narrative_text_tags}
2 
3 The following tokens can be used within Story Text to do things such as change the styling of text or clear the contents of a dialog area on input and much more. You can see this list in the editor by pressing the Tag Help button in the Say command.
4 
5 * {b} Bold Text {/b}
6 * {i} Italic Text {/i}
7 * {color=red} Color Text (color){/color}
8 * {size=30} Text size {/size}
9 * {s}, {s=60} Writing speed (chars per sec){/s}
10 * {w}, {w=0.5} Wait (seconds)
11 * {wi} Wait for input
12 * {wc} Wait for input and clear
13 * {wp}, {wp=0.5} Wait on punctuation (seconds){/wp}
14 * {c} Clear
15 * {x} Exit, advance to the next command without waiting for input
16 * {vpunch=10,0.5} Vertically punch screen (intensity,time)
17 * {hpunch=10,0.5} Horizontally punch screen (intensity,time)
18 * {punch=10,0.5} Punch screen (intensity,time)
19 * {flash=0.5} Flash screen (duration)
20 * {audio=AudioObjectName} Play Audio Once
21 * {audioloop=AudioObjectName} Play Audio Loop
22 * {audiopause=AudioObjectName} Pause Audio
23 * {audiostop=AudioObjectName} Stop Audio
24 * {m=MessageName} Broadcast message
25 * {$VarName} Substitute variable
26 
27 Examples:
28 
29 ```
30 This is a line of dialog.
31 {wc}
32 This is another line of dialog with some {b}bold{/b} styling.
33 This is another line of dialog with some {color=blue}blue{/b} text.
34 ```
